Privacy Policy — Clippara

Effective date: 9 May 2026 · Last updated: 9 May 2026

This privacy policy explains how Clippara ("we", "our", "the app") handles information when you use our service or interact with content we publish on social platforms including Instagram and YouTube.

1. Who We Are

Clippara is an automated short-form video publishing tool operated by Murtuza Merchant, an individual developer based in London, United Kingdom. The app is a personal project that aggregates and publishes short educational and entertainment video content to Instagram and YouTube.

You can contact us at: clippara.media@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

Clippara is not a consumer-facing application. It does not collect, store, or process information from end users or general members of the public. The app operates as a publishing tool authenticated to a single Instagram Business account and a single YouTube channel, both owned by the operator.

The app accesses the following data, all of which belongs to the operator's own accounts:

  • Authentication tokens issued by Meta (Instagram Graph API) and Google (YouTube Data API)
  • Metadata about the operator's own Instagram Business account (account ID, username) required by the API
  • Video files and captions that the operator has chosen to publish

3. How We Use Information

The information accessed by Clippara is used solely to publish video content to the operator's own Instagram and YouTube accounts. No information is shared with third parties, sold, used for advertising, used for analytics on third parties, or used to profile any individual.

4. Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored locally on a private server controlled by the operator, with file system permissions restricted to the operator. Tokens are not transmitted to any third party. Video files are processed in memory or stored temporarily during upload, then deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

Clippara interacts with the following third-party services on the operator's own behalf:

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — for publishing Reels to the operator's Instagram Business account. See Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Google (YouTube) — for publishing videos to the operator's YouTube channel. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic — for generating hashtag suggestions for caption metadata. Only the public caption text is sent; no user data is included. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

The app does not retain user data because it does not collect user data. Authentication tokens are retained for as long as they are needed to operate the app and are deleted when the app is decommissioned or the operator revokes access.

7. Children's Privacy

Clippara is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect or process information from any individual, including children.

8. Your Rights

Because Clippara does not collect personal data from end users, there is no user data to access, correct, or delete. If you believe content published by Clippara on Instagram or YouTube should be removed, please contact us at clippara.media@gmail.com or report the content directly to Instagram or YouTube using their built-in reporting tools.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Material changes will be reflected here and on any associated app listing.

10. Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or about Clippara's data practices, contact: clippara.media@gmail.com
